I, Thelonius Thaddeus, hereby and forthwith declare this, my last will and testament, as such:

The tenth of my wealth, liquid and otherwise, shall be donated to The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints.

The rest shall be divided as such:

To Winifred Thaddeus, I leave 30%.

To George Thaddeus, I leave 20%, to be converted to a government savings bond.

To Augustine Hollister, I leave 40%.

To my beloved wife, Maria Thatcher Thaddeus, I leave the rest, namely, 10%.
